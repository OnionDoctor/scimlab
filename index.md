The Smart Construction and Intelligent Maintenance Laboratory was established in 2021 at the School of Intelligent Civil Engineering and Ocean Engineering, College of Artificial Intelligence, Harbin Institute of Technology Shenzhen. The laboratory primarily focuses on the following areas:


智能建造与智慧运维实验室成立于2021年，位于哈尔滨工业大学深圳智能学部智能土木与海洋工程学院，主要关注以下几个领域：


- Smart and Digital Construction 智能建造、施工信息化方向
- Intelligent Maintanence and Urban renewal 智慧运维、城市更新方向
- Future-oriented Technologies 面向未来的AECO技术
